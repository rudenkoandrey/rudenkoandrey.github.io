Human-centered, socially-aware robotics research

Hi, my name is Andrey Rudenko, I am a PhD candidate at the University of Örebro and Robert Bosch GmbH Corporate Research Renningen. I work on human motion trajectory prediction and predictive planning for service robots. This website contains up-to-date information about my research.
